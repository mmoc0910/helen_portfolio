Challenge

Players struggled with outdated systems and scattered booking sites.

The goal was simple: create one clean, reliable web platform that makes booking a court fast and frustration-free.

01 - Understanding User Pain Points

I spoke with casual players and reviewed competitor to pinpoint blockers.

Users wanted speed, clarity, and real-time availability, without unnecessary steps.

02 - Visual & Strategic Repositioning

I explored multiple design directions to create a fresh, modern, and easy-to-scan interface.

Clean layouts, simple language, and friendly visuals helped Courtly feel more approachable and intuitive.
